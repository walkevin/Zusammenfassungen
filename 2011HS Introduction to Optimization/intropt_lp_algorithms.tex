			& \Downarrow \text{Pivot on (r,s)}\\

                	       & 	& j 		& 	& r &\\\hline
			       &	& \vdots	& 	& \vdots &\\

                	      \end{array}\quad i \in B-s, j\in N-r\\
Proposition 4.6		& \text{
			  \begin{tabular}[t]{lp{14cm}}
			  a. & (Reversibility) The result of a pivot operation of $D'$ on $(s,r)$ is $D$\\
			  b. & (Equivalence) The associated equation systems are equivalent $\Rightarrow V(D) = V(D')$, V denotes the set of solutions\\
			  c. & (Dual equivalence) The associated dual equation systems are equivalent $\Rightarrow V(-D^T) = V(-D'^T)$
